Pasta

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
spaghetti 1 pound
crushed tomatoes 1(28-ounce)
onion chopped

Step 1:

    Cook the pasta.
  1. Bring a large pot of salted water of a rolling boil
  2. Aadd the spaghetti and cook according to the package. Drain the pasta and set it aside.

Step 2:

    Make the Tomato Sauce.
  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet.
  3. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the onion and garlic soften (about 5 minutes).
  4. Pour the crushed tomatoes into the skillet. Bring the sauce to a simmer, then reduce heat and cook for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens slightly.

Step 3: Combine Pasta and Sauce. And serve.
